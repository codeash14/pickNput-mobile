Pick N Put

Help

How to play?

Step 1 : CLick on the item which you want to choose
Step 2 : Click on the box where you want to send your selected item
The position of the item will be changed!!

Rules :

1. Selected item can be only placed over similar items
for eg- red ball can be placed over a red ball only otherwise it will shake to give an error
2. Player have to continue sorting the items until simillar items are placed together
